RE-SCAN LOGS
Re-reads your whole Tarkov log history and re-imports every completed quest it can still find — use this to undo a reset, or if a completed quest is missing. It only adds completions; your manual ticks are kept. (It can only find quests still present in your logs; very old completions Tarkov has since deleted can't be recovered — tick those by hand.)
